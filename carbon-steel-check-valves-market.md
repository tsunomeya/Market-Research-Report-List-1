<p><p>The carbon steel check valves market features two primary types: wafer type and lug type. Wafer type check valves are compact and lightweight, designed to fit between flanges in a pipeline, ensuring easy installation and maintenance. In contrast, lug type check valves have threaded lug connections that allow for easy removal from the pipeline without needing to disconnect the entire system. Both types are crucial for preventing backflow in various industrial applications, contributing to efficiency and safety in fluid management.</p></p>
