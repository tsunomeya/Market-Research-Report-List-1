<p><p>The straightening press market can be divided into manual and automatic types. Manual straightening presses require operator intervention for loading, adjusting, and operating the machinery, offering flexibility but demanding skilled labor. In contrast, automatic straightening presses utilize advanced technology to perform these tasks independently, increasing efficiency and consistency in production. This automation reduces labor costs and minimizes human error, making automatic presses preferable for high-volume operations, while manual presses are often chosen for smaller-scale or specialized tasks.</p></p>
