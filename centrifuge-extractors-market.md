<p><ul><li>Monostage Centrifuge Extractor</li><li>Multistage Centrifuge Extractor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Centrifuge extractors are classified into two main types: monostage and multistage centrifuge extractors. </p><p>Monostage centrifuge extractors operate in a single separation cycle, efficiently extracting materials but often with lower purity levels. In contrast, multistage centrifuge extractors utilize multiple separation cycles, enhancing purity and yield through successive processing stages. This makes multistage systems more suitable for complex extraction processes, such as in the pharmaceutical or food industries, where higher quality and efficiency are paramount for product development.</p></p>
